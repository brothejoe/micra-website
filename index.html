Restoring Coastal Ecosystems While Generating Sustainable Aquaculture Income

MICRA is a scalable model from Aceh, Indonesia integrating shrimp farming, mangrove restoration, and blue carbon finance.

Pilot: 3 hectares | 10 ponds | Expansion target: 30 hectares

About MICRA

Mangrove Integrated Climate Resilient Aquaculture (MICRA) is a circular blue economy model combining aquaculture, ecosystem restoration, and carbon finance.

The Coastal Crisis

Coastal communities in Indonesia are facing declining aquaculture productivity, mangrove deforestation, and increasing climate vulnerability.

Traditional ponds generate low income, high environmental damage, and release methane emissions due to poor waste management.

Our Solution: MICRA

MICRA integrates shrimp farming, mangrove restoration, and blue carbon finance into a single scalable system.

  • ✔ Sustainable shrimp farming (IMTA)
  • ✔ Mangrove restoration buffer
  • ✔ Carbon credit monetization
  • ✔ Renewable energy integration

Proven Model

  • 📍 Location: Aceh, Indonesia
  • 🧬 Existing ponds since 1980s (family-owned)
  • 🌱 Mangrove restoration: 10 hectares
  • 🦐 Pilot: 3 hectares integrated aquaculture

Impact
